So Many Projects! Lots & lots of ideas for summer knitwear are out there. Here are a few to peak your knitting curiosity.



Soft Trails by: Carol Feller

This oversized ladder lace sweater will feel light as a feather to wear. Worked in a single ply wool yarn, it will float gently across any outfit to keep you warm with none of the weight.

Needle: 3.75mm

Pattern is available for purchase on Ravelry $9.90

Fingering weight, 100g, 380m/415yds, needle: 2.25-3.25mm, gauge: 28-32sts = 4ins, hand wash cool, lay flat to dry

Ten beautiful shades to choose from. Very reasonably priced at

$17.29 ea




Summer Knus by Elsebeth Judith

Sommerknus is a round yoke sweater worked from the top down with the possibility to adjust the length of the sleeves (short, ¾ or long sleeves) and the body.The blouse/sweater has an all-over textured pattern.

We are suggesting Berroco's Remix Light for this project

Pay pattern on Ravelry.




Berroco's Remix Light

Fingering weight. 30% nylon, 27% cotton, 24% acrylic, 10% silk, 9% linen. 100g / 3.5oz, 400m/432yds, needle: 3.75mm, gauge: 22sts x 32 rows = 4 ins. Machine wash cold, delicate, lay flat to dry

22 beautiful colours

Big yardage at a great price of $17.29/ball




Cloudburst by: Shania Bilow

Cloudburst is a simple boxy tee with all over eyelet patterning in fingering weight yarn. This versatile top can be layered over dresses or worn with a cami underneath and paired with a flowy skirt or jeans.

The top is worked side to side with two identical pieces for the front and back, with stitches cast on provisionally. After working each piece, the front and back are joined together with a 3-needle bind off and sleeves are worked off the remaining stitches. The shoulders are seamed, leaving an opening for the neck. Stitches are picked up along the bottom to create the bottom ribbed border.

We are suggesting Manos Fino for this pullover

A pay pattern on Ravelry $7.00 USD




Manos Fino

Elegant and refined, Fino combines the softness of merino wool with the luster of silk in a lightweight yarn that’s perfect for heirloom accessories or luxurious garments. Fino features a new dye process that yields complex, subtle, tonal colors.

70% merino wool, 30% silk, 100g, 490yds, 450m, gauge: 24-28sts/4ins, needles: 3-3.75mm, handwash in cool water and dry flat

Huge yardage. 4 gorgeous shades.

PROJECT IDEA #8 - The Boulevard Blanket

The Boulevard Blanket….it’s quick, easy, chunky, warm and reversible! At first glance you might think this pattern is just simple garter stitch. Look again!
An easy K3, P3 repeat pattern creates a subtle striping effect. The name of this pattern is inspired by the beautiful tree lined boulevards of my hometown….Kansas City.
Free pattern by: Fifty Four Ten Studio on Ravelry. Excellent beginner project. Nicer than just plain garter stitch but still fast to knit - https://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/the-boulevard-blanket.
Level Beginner
Sizes: Baby to 2XL (6sizes)

Materials
- superbulky yarn
- check pattern for quantities of main colour depending upon size
- Needles: 9.0mm x 32 inches
- Gauge: 18.5sts/14.5 rows = 4in in garter stitch

PROJECT IDEA #7 - Novice Sweater Junior

The Novice Sweater Junior is knit seamlessly from the top down. That means that you will begin by knitting the neckband, then the yoke, then the body, and finally the sleeves. The sweater is knit in stockinette stitch, while the neckband, bottom edge (waist band), and cuffs are knit in rib.
Purchase pattern by: PetiteKnit on Ravelry. Excellent beginner project. She used lace weight + fingering + fingering to achieve chunky weight. You can just use a chunky yarn to make the knitting easier. - https://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/novice-sweater-junior.
Level Advanced beginner
Sizes: 1-2yrs to 12-14yrs
Materials
- check pattern for quantities of main colour depending upon size
- Needles: 5.5mm & 6.0mm varying lengths for neckline, body, sleeves & cuffs
- Gauge: 16sts/21 rows = 4in in stocking stitch

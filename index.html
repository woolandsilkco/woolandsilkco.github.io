Featured Project

Pleasant Trip Cowl

By Laura Aylor

  • Knitted in Universal Yarns Little Bird
  • a lovely comfortable cowl that can be worn long or doubled up. Definitely at least a 3 season wearable addition to your wardrobe.
  • in-store sample knitted using only 1 ball of Little Bird in #110 New Leaf (which makes it not quite as high), takes 2 for full pattern height of 10 inches
  • a very nice sport weight yarn with the feel of pima cotton or bamboo but the surprising reality of 100% Acrylic. New into store you will be amazed by this yarn!
  • done on 3.5mm 80cm circular needle
  • Pattern $4.00 USD

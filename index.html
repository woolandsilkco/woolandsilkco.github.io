Pretty Panels Cardigan

  • a lovely cardigan with just a little bit of interest at the front
  • body of the sweater is in Vintage DK #2189 Charcoal
  • front panels are knitted by holding 1 strand of Misti Alpaca Hand Paint Sock #HS43 Eiffel together with 1 strand of Drops Kid-Silk #04 Medium Pink
  • top-down continuous construction means no seams!
  • finished with a picot edge around neck, down front & edges of sleeves
  • this pattern is currently under construction and will be published on Ravelry when knitting is completed

Stripezoid Shawl

  • 1 skein of Madeline Tosh Light (Amber Trinket)
  • 4.0mm x 80cm circular needle
  • skill level - intermediate

Age of Brass & Steam by Orange Flower Yarn (Helen)

  • An easy to follow pattern with occasional bands of openwork.
  • Excellent way to showcase this yarn.
  • You can continue to knit this shawl (leaving enough yarn to cast off), until you run out of yarn.
  • 2 balls of Knitting Fever Painted Mist (#301 Venice Carnival)
  • free pattern
  • 5.0mm x 80cm circular needle
  • skill level - easy

Classes

Thu Jan 08 2015 - Learn to Knit

This class has already begun in December but you are welcome to join in for the last 2 parts in January. Created for anyone who is new to knitting we cover casting on, basic knit & purl stitches & casting off. The project will be a sampler scarf.

Thu Jan 15 2015 - Learn to Crochet

For those just wanting to learn how to crochet. Class started in December but 2 lessons offered in January to anyone wanting to join. Cover beginning chain, foundation row, single, double & treble crochet. Project can be a scarf (2 choices) or a lap blanket.

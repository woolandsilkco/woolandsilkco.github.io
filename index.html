Featured Project

"Frolic Pullover"

by Andrea Cull

Knitted in tonal fingering weight yarn this will certainly be a go to in your wardrobe. Bottom up & in the round makes it a very sensible project.

The Frolic Pullover features a elegant lace yoke paired with playful ruched sleeves and a simple neckline. It can easily be dressed up or worn with your favorite jeans. Additionally the lightweight fabric is offers versatility so it can be worn indoors or layered for wear in many seasons and climates.

Pair this pattern with our featured yarn Ella Rae Lace Merino - currently 30% off in store (4 skeins) to make up to a size large (42-1/4" bust). worn with 2" of positive ease

What We're Knitting/Crocheting

Mandara T-top by: Susan Mills (Diane)
  • Cropped cotton T-top knitted in a loose twist cotton to make a lighter weight garment. Knitted bottom up.
  • In Drops Belle XS took 3 balls #06 Silver, 2 balls #13 Dk Jeans Blue
  • We went 3.75mm (instead of 4.0mm) to get gauge.
  • Pattern available in-store or on Ravelry
  • Skill level - advanced beginner

Sandy Cardigan by: Kiri FitzGerald (Leeanne)
  • Easy to wear wardrobe staple with a pretty all-over boxy lace pattern.
  • Uses multiple needle sizes to decrease lace for A-line shape without altering your lace pattern.
  • Ella Rae Rustic Silk - sample in colour 07 Niagara Falls
  • Pattern available in the Knit Scene Summer 2018 magazine
  • Skill level - intermediate

Full & Half Sail shawl by: Bantry Yarns (Diane)
  • A lovely lightweight triangular shawl. Completed using 2 very different yarns to great effect.
  • Louisa Harding Pittura (sample 623 Guenica - 1 ball)
  • Drops Kid-Silk (sample 09 Pearl Grey - 2 balls)
  • Pattern in-store or on Ravelry 4.00 Euros
  • Skill level - easy

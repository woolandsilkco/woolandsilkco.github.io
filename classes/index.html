Summer Classes ! Sock Makers & Shawl Builders
 As a change of pace we have decided to offer project classes for sock and shawl knitters for the summer. You need to already be able to knit socks and have experience with shawls for these classes. Each project is 1 or 2 dates only. Some require knitting homework before the class so please read the descriptions carefully. Prices are all inclusive. Yarn(you choose the colour we choose the yarn type), pattern, lesson(s) and beads if required. (plus the HST of course). Needles are not provided or included. Classes begin in July so look them over & get signed up now! Click on each photo to see the Ravelry pattern page or go to http://www.woolandsilkco.com (click on "Classes" at the top of the page).

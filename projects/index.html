Kukka Cardigan

The Kukka Cardigan Chunky is a classic open front cardigan. It is cut closely to the body but not tight-ly and, knitted from fluffy yarns, fits wonderfully under a winter jacket. If you work the cardigan in a fluffy, light yarn, it is perfect for the transition time in spring and autumn. The Kukka Cardigan Chunky is worked flat top down with raglan increases. The sleeves are worked in rounds with the Magic Loop method, double pointed needles or any other needle system of your choice. The front bands are worked with slip stitches and create the typical “Kukka look” together with the easy to remember rice grain pattern.
Purchase pattern by: rosa p. on Ravelry. Worked top down & in the round seamlessly. You can knit it in a chunky yarn, with or without added mohair. Or in a soft chunky like Estelle Drift. - https://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/kukka-cardigan-chunky.
Level Intermediate (or advanced beginner)
Sizes: XS to 5XL
Materials
- check pattern for quantities of main colour depending upon size (766-1640 yds)
- Needles: 6.0mm & 6.5mm varying lengths for neckline, body, sleeves & cuffs
- Gauge: 14sts/23 rows = 4in in stocking stitch

Orbits

Orbits is the fourth design in Rachel Illsley's Einaudi Collection – a collection of knitwear patterns inspired by pieces of music written by Ludovico Einaudi. Orbits features stranded colourwork through the majority of the yoke and into the upper body and sleeves, with patterns that capture the mysterious, repetitive yet delicate essence of the music.
Purchase pattern on Ravelry - https://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/orbits.
Worked top down & in the round. Pick 2 colours of beautiful fingering yarn to knit this gorgeous sweater. If you use a tonal fingering for the colourwork it will provide some nice variation.
Level Intermediate (or advanced beginner)
Sizes: XS to 5XL
Materials
- 2 colours of fingering weight yarn (only 1 skein of contrast colour for all sizes)
- check pattern for quantities of main colour depending upon size
- Needles: 2.75mm & 3.25mm varying lengths for neckline, body, sleeves & cuffs
- Gauge: 24sts/38 rows = 4in in stocking stitch

Timbo Vest (by Gabriella Calderini)

Another project idea has found us. Winter series. The Timbo Vest is a simple yet interesting knit, suitable for beginners and fast enough to become your go-to gift project. It is worked from top down in an easy broken rib stitch, with a stockinette shoulder detail. A free pattern on Ravelry. We are currently working on a sample for in the store. Decided to make the neckline just a few rows instead of the full turtleneck style. Knitted in chunky yarn it will move along quickly for you. I love the idea of it keeping my body warm while allowing my arms to be free. Make it as long or as short as you wish! Free pattern on Ravelry! Worked top down. Is in the round after armhole sections are completed. Make it in 2 colours or one solid. Either will look nice. We have chosen our new Eco Tweed chunky to knit the sample.

Level - advanced beginner

Sizes: XS to XL

Materials

- chunky weight yarn
- check pattern for quantities depending upon size
- Needles: 6.5mm in 40, 60 & 80(or 100)cm circular (all 3 lengths required)
- Gauge: 15sts/22 rows = 4in in broken rib pattern
